About Me

I'm a hands-on audio and tech enthusiast working at the intersection of music and software. From writing and recording songs to building tools that help with sound design, I like exploring practical and creative ways to work with sound and code.

I use tools like REAPER, Arduino, Max/MSP, and the Web Audio API, and I enjoy tinkering with MIDI gear, circuits, and interactive controls for music.
